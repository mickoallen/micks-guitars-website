About Mick's Guitars

My Story

I've been playing guitar for 25 years, and now I build them by hand, one at a time. This isn't my day job, it's something I do because I genuinely enjoy it. No rush, no pressure, just taking the time each guitar deserves.

Every guitar I make is different, and each one gets my full attention. Nothing leaves my workbench unless I'm genuinely happy with it. I take pride in what I make, and I play every instrument I build. There's something cool about creating something with your hands and then actually using it to make music.

My Approach

Most guitars take around 4-6 months to complete. These are handmade guitars, if you want something from a factory, just head to any music store. If you're looking for something one-of-a-kind, made by someone who takes their time and knows what makes a guitar work, let's chat.
